Saber

Score: 0 Lives: 3

Hit a ball fast enough to reduce its health. Hit it enough times to destroy it and get a point.

If a ball touches the saber's hilt, you lose a life. The game ends when all lives are lost. Things get more fun over time.

Touch controls: Touch and drag to move the saber.

Mouse controls: Move the mouse to the center of the screen to take up your saber. Click and hold to stop swinging.
